Vicor’s desktop and mobile product filtering

Vicor Corporation needed to transform their desktop product filtering to a mobile-ready experience without sacrificing functionality. This involved redesigning the unintuitive existing product filtering and envisioning an experience that would fit the size constraints of the mobile platform. View Case Study ↗

The Garage at Microsoft Internship

During a 4-month internship at The Garage, my group was entrusted to design a device to track and control an Iced-Coffee keg. Our prototype of the device uses a ball valve to control the flow of the liquid, an RFID reader to track the users, and a data base to store the different visits. View Case Study ↗
